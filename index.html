Stéphane Krims

Welcome to my official website! I am a musician currently based in Tiohtià:ke, a.k.a. Montréal, QC. While I am generally known as a bassist, I play many different instruments and always love working with them all in as many different contexts as possible. I've had the privilege of having received education from many world-renowned sources - including a BMus in Jazz Bass from McGill - as well as sharing the stage with some world-class artists in both professional and educational settings.

On this website you will find descriptions of all the services I provide, the recordings you can listen to, and any shows I have coming up. I've also made an anti-oppression statement. If you'd like to get in touch with me, just head over to the Contact page and I will get back to you as soon as I can.

Thank you!

Stéphane
