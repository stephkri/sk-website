My Services

As a musician, I can offer a wide variety of services at a professional level.

Performing

While I am primarily known as a bassist (both upright and electric), I play a wide variety of instruments at a level where I could perform in a professional setting. Indeed, many of the instruments in my full list can be heard in the recordings listed in my discography.

+ Full list of instruments I play

  • Double bass (both arco and pizz, C extension available)*
  • Electric bass (multiple tonal options, low B available)*
  • Guitar (including nylon-string, ERG, 12-string and lap steel)*
  • Cello*
  • Mandolin*
  • Charango*
  • Keyboards (including piano, synthesizer and organ)
  • Drums and percussion (including full kit, congas and various toys)
  • Trumpet
  • Trombone
  • Fiddle (not violin)
  • Ukulele*
  • Banjo
  • Recorder
  • Tuba

    • All instruments marked * can be taught at a professional level.

Teaching

I can teach instruments marked with an asterisk (in the above list) at a professional level, and all other instruments at a beginner level.

I am well-versed in the following styles:

  • Western classical music
  • Folk
  • Funk
  • Country
  • Rock

I can also teach these fundamental concepts: such as theory, ear training, sight reading, composition, improvisation and arrangement.

  • Theory
  • Ear training
  • Sight reading
  • Composition
  • Improvisation
  • Arrangement

Transcription

I am particularly adept at transcription, and can easily transcribe any audio recording needed as long as it can be written down with traditional Western sheet music.

Arranging

I have a thorough knowledge of most well-known instruments, their ranges and capabilities, and how they interact with each other. I can arrange any song you need - or compose my own song - and make it as simple or as complex as necessary.
